The rules

Each team had 3 months to film 8 minute edits in ordered to be premiered online (initially set to be shown at the WMC in Compiegne, which was pushed back to 2021).

The contestants

The contest was extended to a handful of teams:

  • António Macieira (Portugal)
  • Yuya Nakayama, Maaken, Champ Okada (Japan)
  • AJ Lawson and Jason Lee (USA)
  • Sonia Nicolau and Erica Pintea (Romania)
