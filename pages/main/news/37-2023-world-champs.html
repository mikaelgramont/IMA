Boardercross and Freestyle - July 12th/15th

It's already been 6 years since the AMC hosted the WMC, and if you were there you remember how great of an event that was.

Well, the AMC has been working very hard for the past few years to bring you an even better competition, with a much improved boardercross track and freestyle areas.

Location

The AMC's amazing mountainboard park is located in Venette.

Schedule

The event is spread over 4 days starting on Wednesday the 12th, but registration will be open on site Tuesday night.

  • 10h-18h: Registration & training
  • 12h-14h: Lunch

  • 16h-18h: Freestyle qualifiers

Thursday, July 13th

Downhill - July 17th/18th

If you've been to a World Mountainboard Championship before, chances are you've met Veteran mountainboard athlete Vincent Lavaud ("Cambione"). AS it turns out, he's has long had his mind set on hosting a great Downhill competition in his home region of Sancy in central France. And that's just what he's doing this year!

On the heels of the Boardercross and Freestyle competitions a few days earlier, the Downhill championship is going to blow minds with an amazing location, and chairlift access. There will also be a freestyle demo and learn to ride sessions for beginners and tourists.

Location

Super Besse is located in the Auvergne region in central France. More info on the event website.

Check out the event website for more info on the track!

Schedule
