SEPARATOR

As you all know, we've had to cancel the 2021 World Mountainboard Championships again this year due to the health risks and to the financial risk of running a low-turn out event for the organizers, the Assocation Mountainboard Compiègne (AMC).

However, the great news is that the AMC and L.A. Mountainboard Park clubs are setting up a week-long summer camp at their two parks! Both are world-class mountainboard parks, and if you're looking for an opportunity to train there and at other locations, you should definitely attend!

Below are details provided by them.

The Camp will be mostly about chill and ride with friends at the L.A Mountainboard Park near Nantes, from July 10th to the 13th, then transfer to Compiègne to enjoy the spot and the work done for the canceled WMC21.

Schedule and info for L.A. Mountainboard Park

It is still a little early to confirm this, but we’ll try to organise a session at Abbaretz and the Skatepark Le Hangar (those who've followed L.A Mountainboard for long time should know what we mean).

More info in the weeks to come, so save your dates and stay tuned.

Fees: 10€

Food: BBQ at your disposal to cook and food truck on site the evening (if enough people are interested)

Facilities: camping with shower on site, beautiful hike from the spot, kayak available to navigate the «down the spot» river, slacklines, 20 min from seaside

Shuttle to Compiègne depending on numbers of participants (arrangement with other riders or transfer by bus)
