The IMA and Australian Mountainboarders are happy to announce that the 2022 World Mountainboard Championship are scheduled for Dec 14th to 18th at The Ranch, in Victoria, Australia.

For the first time ever, the WMC will take a break from being held in the extended European region, and will take place in the southern hemisphere!
More details will be given in time.

As you might expect, this is all under the condition that travelling is allowed at the time of the event.
