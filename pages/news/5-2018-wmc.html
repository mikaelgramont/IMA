SEPARATOR

World Mountainboard Championship 2018 (June 29th-30th) - live coverage

We'll be adding updates as the event takes place, so come back often!

As a reminder, qualifiers are happening Friday 29th, and finals are on Saturday 30th.
For more details, check out the timeline on the official event site.

  • Latest update - June 30th - 20:52

    WMC18 is a wrap, we're all finished with racing and freestyle and our queens and kings were crowned!
We'll have detailed results tomorrow, as for now, riders are heading to the dinner and after-party.

We did have a few injured riders, and so of course our thoughts are with them. The mountainboarding community is tight and we know everyone will take care of their friends.

Thank you everyone who worked so hard to make this happen, the organisers and all the volunteers. A big thank you to all the riders who made it here in Kranj, Slovenia and made this event so special. We can't wait to see you next year for the next WMC - location to be determined! But for now, the party is ON!

    Mika

  • Update - June 30th - 18:30

    It's 18:30, and pro freestyle finals riders are into their last run (out of 5).
Everything is still up in the air, but we've seen come cool stuff, and as always it's a great show.

Right after that's over, we'll be crowning the winners and then moving into dinner.

About that, rider dinner and party are starting at 20:30 at Gostilna Stari Mayr in Kranj, so we'll see you there for food and prize giving.

    Mika

  • Update - June 30th - 14:44

    Boardercross is done for 2018! We'll be posting results soon, but you should know this: let's just say there was some change. Early results here!

    Mika

  • Update - June 30th - 08:54

    Alright, we have a big day ahead of us today, and the weather is going to be great!

Remember, riders meeting at the top of the track at 10:00 sharp, with boardercross finals starting at 11:00. Freestyle will start later, we'll keep you posted throughout the day.

At the end of the day today, we'll have our new World Champions for 2018, good luck everyone!

    Mika

  • Update - June 29th - 21:00

    June 30th - nd that's a wrap on Qualifying day! The Pro Freestyle Qualifying results are available here.

What started out as a rainy, grey day turned into gorgeous sunshine and fantastic mountainboarding. We can't wait for Finals day tomorrow - athletes meeting at 10am, racing starting at 11!

    Matt

  • Update - June 29th - 16:50

    Qualifying for BoarderX is done! Super fast racing and some very close finished - you can see the results here.

We've also got the first round of Knockout races ready for tomorrow morning which you can see here.

We move on to Pro Freestyle Qualifying at 6!

    Matt

  • Update - June 29th - 13:05

    Aaaaand the very first qualifying race just started, it's about to get real!

    Mika

    Unfortunately we've been hit by rain this morning so we've delayed qualifying for now.

We sent a test run down the track just now but it's still too slippery so we are going to keep working on getting it dry.

Athlete safety is our priority so we'll only start the competition when it is safe. The sun is shining now so we don't think it'll be long ðŸ¤ž

  • Update - June 29th - 08:20

    The Qualifying races are in! You can check them out here.

    Matt

  • Update - June 29th - 07:43

    Today's the day! The contest starts today for real with qualifications for both freestyle and boardercross. We'll see everyone on the hill at 10:00 sharp!

We premiered the videos for the 4 Down video contest last night during dinner at the hotel Bellevue and it was a success! All videos were very different from each other and you could tell they made people want to ride. Congrats to the athletes and filmmakers, mission accomplished!

    Mika

  • Update - June 28th - 17:47

    To the people attending the event:
- everyone get to the Hotel Bellevue in Kranj! https://goo.gl/maps/o8ZMUE74cQ22
- registration for the competition is from 18:00 to 20:00
- the premiere of the 4 Down video contest will be at 20:00
- dinner on site at 20:30

    Mika

  • Update - June 28th - 16:39

    Practice is in full swing and the track and jumps are riding really well!
Registration is at Hotel Bellevue tonight from 6pm, including dinner and the 4 down video contest!

    Matt

  • Update - June 28th - 09:32

     We had some rain overnight and the sky is looking grey but we think it'll really help the track. It was getting dusty and crumbly and this bit of rain will help to settle the dust and pull the track together.

The wind is strong so we're expecting the track to dry pretty quickly, then we'll be getting on with the last day of practice before the competition starts tomorrow!

    Matt

  • Update - June 27th - 19:48

    Training officially starts tomorrow (Thursday) but more and more riders have been practising this afternoon and the track is looking gooood! As usual, it's getting faster, and though we expect some rain tomorrow, it will make for some fun races to watch.

A few athletes did some test runs on the freestyle, and again, as usual, it needs some adjustments (water drainage for example), but the important things are there: the jump setup is good and will allow for some big tricks!

    Mika

  • Update - June 27th - 09:14

    Riders keep pouring into the Bellevue hotel which sits above the track further down the valley. The energy is building up!

The freestyle section still needs some work, which we're hoping to put in today. Stay tuned!

    Mika

  • Update - June 26th - 22:05

    A great day at the site - we are still recovering from the heavy downpours and storms over the weekend but we are definitely getting there, with the track rideable top to bottom at the end of the day.

The Dirt Dessert team have worked super hard over the last few months and their hard work has been well received by the athletes, all of them riding with giant smiles on their faces and thoroughly enjoying the track.

Tomorrow's plan is to get the Freestyle line working now that it's had time to dry out some more and fix the last few things on the BoarderX, then we'll be set for official practice on Thursday!

    Matt

  • Update - June 26th - 15:33

    Our lunchtime project is finished and the hip had been re-faced!

Quick break in the shade then back on to some more spade work ðŸ’ª

Rumour has it, the President will arrive soon....

    Matt

  • Update - June 26th - 10:52

    The Sun is shining and the weather is great!

The track is starting to dry out which makes it the perfect time to get a spade in your hand.

Our plan for the morning is to add some extra features for extra fun! They won't make the racing faster, but they'll make the practice runs extra fun!

    Matt

  • Update - June 25th - 23:55

    The very first athletes are starting to show up, and it will soon look like the annual WMC gathering we've all come to love.
In the meantime, the rain this morning meant that the track was wet and athletes retreated to Kranj's pumptrack.

The track is looking great by the way! The Dirt Dessert team has put in a ton of work (and not just on the track, but also the surrounding areas), and we're looking forward to the event!
We'll be putting finishing touches over the next couple of days, and then it's on!

The forecast does call for some rain showers until Thursday, but has us in the clear with nice weather and high temperatures for the actual competition!

If you're coming, bring bug spray (watch out for mosquitoes and ticks!) as well as sunscreen! See you soon!

    Mika
